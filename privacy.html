Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

1. Data We Collect

This Seat Booking App is a demonstration project. We do not store your personal information on any external servers. All data is stored locally on your device.

2. Use of LocalStorage

We use your browser's localStorage to provide essential features, including:

  • Saving your selected movie and service preferences.
  • Tracking booked and reserved seats in the screening room.
  • Maintaining your order history within the current session.

3. Data Retention

Data remains in your browser's localStorage until you manually clear your browser cache or we programmatically reset the application state.

4. Your Rights

Since all data is local, you have full control over it. You can delete all information associated with this app at any time by clearing your browser data or using the "Reset" functions within the app interface.

5. Compliance

This policy is designed to comply with basic transparency requirements regarding data usage in web applications.
